description: Explain a medical condition or concept in patient-friendly language
---

Create a patient-friendly explanation of: $ARGUMENTS

Write as if explaining to someone with no medical background. Use:
- Simple, everyday language (6th-8th grade reading level)
- Analogies and comparisons to familiar things
- Short sentences and paragraphs
- A warm, reassuring tone

Structure:
1. **What it is** (simple definition)
2. **What causes it** (in everyday terms)
3. **What it feels like** (common symptoms)
4. **How doctors find it** (diagnosis process)
5. **How it's treated** (options in plain terms)
6. **What you can do** (self-care, lifestyle)
7. **Questions to ask your doctor**
8. **When to get help right away** (red flags)

Offer to create a downloadable handout (Word or PDF) if the user wants a document they can share or print.
