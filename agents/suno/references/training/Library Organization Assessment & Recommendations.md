## Assessment: What Needs Improvement

### Issue 1: Version Clutter (HIGH PRIORITY)
Your biggest organizational challenge is **massive version accumulation**. With ~581 songs across workspaces but only ~15-20 unique song projects, you're averaging **30+ iterations per song**. While iteration is great for creative refinement, without cleanup the library becomes overwhelming.

**The problem:** When you open "Rocket To Moon" workspace (48 songs), which version is the best one? Which are dead ends? Which was the one you liked at 2 AM last Tuesday?

**Recommendation:**
- For each workspace, identify the **1-3 best versions** and mark them (star them, rename with "BEST" or "FINAL")
- Consider trashing clearly inferior early drafts — you can always regenerate
- Use a naming convention like: `Song Name - V1 (Genre) - BEST` vs `Song Name - V12 (test)`

### Issue 2: No "Best Of" or "Portfolio" Playlist (HIGH PRIORITY)
You have 2 playlists total. You need at minimum:
- **"Best Versions"** — Your top 1-2 picks from each song project
- **"Portfolio / Showcase"** — Songs you'd share publicly or send to people
- **"Work In Progress"** — Songs actively being refined
- **"Genre Sampler"** — Best songs organized by genre for quick reference

### Issue 3: No Personas Created (MEDIUM PRIORITY)
Your Personas tab is empty. Given you have 20+ song projects with distinct vocal styles, you should be creating Personas from your best songs to maintain vocal consistency. This is especially relevant since voice cloning is in your Day 5 lesson plan.

**Quick wins:**
- Create a Persona from your best "Champagne In The Glass" vocal
- Create a Persona from your best "Rocket To Moon" vocal
- Create a Persona from your best "Go Back In Time" vocal
- Experiment with your own voice (Day 5 lesson!)

### Issue 4: Inconsistent Naming Conventions (MEDIUM PRIORITY)
Current naming is inconsistent:
- `CITG Claude V4` (abbreviation + AI tool + version)
- `Go Back In Time - Demo - 2_5_26` (full name + date)
- `Trouble Bad finally` (informal)
- `Backing vocals` (no song context)
- `CITG GPT Claude V1` vs `CITG T2 V1` (multiple abbreviation schemes)

**Recommended naming convention:**
```
[Song Name] - [Version] - [Variant] - [Status]

Examples:
Champagne In The Glass - V3 - Arena Rock - BEST
Rocket To Moon - V7 - Synthwave - WIP
Go Back In Time - V2 - Acoustic Demo - FINAL
```

### Issue 5: "Prompt Test" Workspace (LOW PRIORITY)
Having 54 songs in a "Prompt Test" workspace is fine for experimentation, but consider:
- Moving any genuinely good songs to their proper song workspace
- Archiving/trashing the rest periodically
- Keeping this workspace small and cleaned out regularly

### Issue 6: "My Workspace" is a Catch-all (LOW PRIORITY)
The default "My Workspace" (15 songs) likely contains miscellaneous items. Review and redistribute these to their proper song workspaces.
